How to Make the Most of Your 5-Day Window

Here are our recommendations for getting the most out of your extended rental period:

  1. Schedule delivery 2 days before your event. This gives you a full day to add decorations, test your layout, and make adjustments.
  2. Do a walkthrough the evening before. Check that all tables are positioned where you want them, all chairs are in place, and the tent is exactly right.
  3. Take photos before guests arrive. Your setup will never look cleaner than the morning of your event. Capture it.
  4. Schedule pickup for the day after your event. You deserve a morning to sleep in, not a 7am teardown session. We will come get everything when you are ready.
  5. Enjoy the flexibility. If plans change, weather shifts, or you decide to move a table, you have days to handle it calmly.
