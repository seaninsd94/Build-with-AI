What "White-Glove" Actually Means

In the event rental industry, there is a wide range of delivery service levels. Some companies drop items at your curb and drive away. Others charge extra for setup. At Tasteful Event Rentals, white-glove service is not an upgrade or add-on. It is the standard experience every customer receives, included in your rental price.

White-glove service means:

  • We deliver everything directly to your event location, not just the curb
  • We unload, carry, and position every item where you want it
  • We fully set up tents, tables, and chairs according to your layout
  • We return after your event to break everything down and haul it away
  • You never lift a finger

The Full Experience, Step by Step

1

Booking & Scheduling

After you place your order through our online catalogue or by calling (858) 255-1130, we confirm your event date, delivery address, and rental items. We then schedule a delivery window that works for your timeline. Because of our 5-day rental period, we can deliver 1 to 2 days before your event so everything is ready when you need it.

2

Pre-Delivery Communication

The day before delivery, we confirm the delivery time window with you via text or phone. If you have specific placement instructions, a site map, or venue requirements, this is when we finalize those details. We want zero surprises on delivery day, for you or for us.

3

Delivery Day Arrival

Our team arrives within your scheduled delivery window with everything loaded, inspected, and ready to go. We bring all items directly to your setup location, whether that is a backyard, a park pavilion, a community center, or a private venue anywhere in San Diego County, Los Angeles County, or Palm Springs.

4

Full Setup & Arrangement

This is where the magic happens. Our crew does not just drop off items and leave. We fully set up every piece:

  • Tents: Erected, staked or weighted, and checked for stability
  • Tables: Unfolded, positioned, and leveled at your desired locations
  • Chairs: Opened and arranged around tables or in rows for ceremonies
  • Linens: Draped and smoothed on each table
  • Games & photo booth: Assembled and placed in your preferred area
5

Walkthrough & Approval

Before we leave, we walk through the setup with you (or your designated contact). If you want any tables shifted, chairs rearranged, or a tent repositioned, we handle it on the spot. We do not leave until you are completely satisfied with the layout.

6

Your Event

This is the part where you relax and enjoy. Everything is set up, sturdy, and looking great. If anything comes up during your event, you have our direct phone number for support.

7

Pickup & Removal

After your event, we schedule a pickup within your 5-day rental window. Our team returns, breaks down all equipment, loads everything onto our truck, and leaves your space exactly as we found it. No folding chairs to stack, no tent to wrestle with, no tables to haul. It is all handled.

Why This Matters for Your Event

When you are planning an event in San Diego, the logistics of setting up equipment can be one of the most stressful parts. We hear it from customers all the time: the event itself is fun, but the hours before and after, hauling, assembling, arranging, and cleaning up, are exhausting.

Our white-glove service eliminates that entire burden. Here is what our clients gain:

  • Time: Instead of spending 3 to 5 hours on setup and teardown, you spend zero. Use that time for decorating, cooking, greeting guests, or simply taking a deep breath before the party starts.
  • Energy: Setting up tents and tables is physical work. Arriving at your own event already tired and sweaty is not the experience you want.
  • Confidence: When a professional team sets up your tent, you know it is safe and stable. When we arrange your tables, they are level and properly spaced. No wobbling, no guesswork.
  • Appearance: Professional setup simply looks better. Evenly spaced chairs, properly draped linens, and a cleanly erected tent create a polished atmosphere that guests notice.
Pro tip: If you have a specific table layout in mind (U-shape, banquet rows, rounds with dance floor, etc.), share it with us when you book. We will set it up exactly to your plan. Not sure what layout works best? We are happy to recommend arrangements based on your guest count and venue. Check our FAQ page for common layout questions.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to be home for the delivery and setup?

Someone needs to be present to show our team where to set up and approve the final layout. This can be you, a family member, a friend, or a venue coordinator. If you cannot be there, just designate a contact person and let us know in advance.

How long does setup take?

Setup time depends on the size of your order. A typical 50-guest setup with tables, chairs, and a tent takes our team about 30 to 45 minutes. Larger events with 100+ guests and multiple tents may take 1 to 2 hours. Either way, we build this into our delivery schedule so everything is ready well before your event.

Is there an extra charge for delivery and setup?

No. White-glove delivery, full setup, and post-event pickup are included in every rental from Tasteful Event Rentals. There are no hidden delivery fees or setup surcharges. The price you see on our catalogue is the price you pay. Call (858) 255-1130 with any questions.
