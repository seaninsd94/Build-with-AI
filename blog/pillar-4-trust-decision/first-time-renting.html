First Time Renting Event Equipment? Here's Exactly What to Expect

If you have never rented tables, chairs, or a tent for an event before, the process might feel unfamiliar. You probably have questions: How does it work? What if something gets damaged? Is there a minimum order? This guide walks you through the entire rental process from start to finish so you know exactly what to expect. No surprises, no confusion, just a simple path to a great event.

1

Browse the Catalogue

Everything starts at our online rental catalogue. You can browse all available items from your phone or computer, any time of day. Here is what you will find:

  • Tents - Pop-up tents (10x10, 10x20) and marquee/festival tents for larger events. Starting at $95.
  • Chairs - White folding chairs ($1.95 each) and padded resin chairs ($3.95 each).
  • Tables - 6ft rectangular ($9.50) and 60" round ($12.50) tables.
  • Linens - Tablecloths in multiple colors ($8.50 each).
  • Extras - Yard games ($15-$35) and a photo booth ($195).

Each item has photos, dimensions, and pricing listed. If you are not sure how many tables or chairs you need, our FAQ page has helpful guidelines based on guest count.

2

Get a Quote or Book Online

Once you know what you need, you have two options:

  • Book online instantly. Add items to your cart on our Booqable-powered booking system, select your event date and delivery address, and check out. It takes about 5 minutes. Here is how to book step by step.
  • Call for a personalized quote. If you prefer talking to a person, or if you have questions about what you need, call us at (858) 255-1130. We are available Monday through Friday, 9am to 5pm, and Saturdays by appointment. We will walk you through the options and give you an exact price.

There is no pressure either way. Many first-time renters prefer to call and talk through their event. That is completely fine and we enjoy it.

3

Confirm Your Order

After placing your order, you will receive a confirmation with:

  • A complete list of your rental items
  • Your event date and delivery address
  • Your total price (all-inclusive, no hidden fees)
  • Your 5-day rental window (delivery and pickup dates)

If you need to make changes after booking, such as adding more chairs or switching table sizes, just call us. We are flexible and happy to adjust your order as long as inventory is available.

4

Delivery Day

This is where our white-glove delivery service kicks in. Here is what happens:

  • We confirm the delivery time with you the day before
  • Our team arrives within your scheduled window
  • We carry every item to your setup location (backyard, park, venue, wherever)
  • We fully set up tents, open and arrange tables and chairs, drape linens, and assemble games
  • We do a walkthrough with you to make sure everything looks right
  • We leave only when you are happy with the setup

Thanks to our 5-day rental period, delivery usually happens 1 to 2 days before your event. That means you have time to add decorations, test your layout, and relax before the big day.

5

Your Event

Everything is set up, looking great, and ready for guests. Your only job is to enjoy the event you planned. If anything comes up, you have our direct phone number. But in our experience, once the setup is done, things run smoothly.

A few tips for event day:

  • Take photos of the setup before guests arrive, it will never look that clean again
  • If it rains unexpectedly, your tent has you covered (literally)
  • Let guests know the games and photo booth are there, they sometimes go unnoticed at first but become the life of the party once people discover them
6

Pickup

After your event, we schedule a pickup within your 5-day rental window. Our team returns, breaks down everything, loads it onto our truck, and leaves your space clean. You do not need to fold a single chair or take down the tent.

For most clients, pickup happens the morning after the event or the following day. No rush. No late fees within your rental window.

Common First-Timer Concerns (Answered Honestly)

We hear these questions from nearly every first-time renter. Here are straight answers:

"What if something gets damaged during my event?"

Normal wear and tear is expected and included. A spill on a tablecloth, a scuff on a chair, that is all part of normal event use and you will not be charged for it. We only charge for damage that goes beyond normal use, like a broken table leg or a torn tent. This rarely happens, and we will always discuss it with you before any charges.

"What if the weather changes?"

San Diego weather is generally cooperative, but surprises happen. If you have a tent, you are covered for light rain and strong sun. If severe weather forces you to cancel or postpone your event, call us as early as possible. We will work with you to reschedule your delivery at no extra cost whenever we can.

"Is there a minimum order?"

We do not have a strict minimum order, but our delivery and setup are most cost-effective for events of 20+ guests. If you are planning something smaller, call us at (858) 255-1130 and we will figure out the best option for you.

"What is your cancellation policy?"

We understand that plans change. If you need to cancel, let us know as far in advance as possible. We will walk you through the specific terms when you book. Our goal is to be fair and flexible, not to penalize you for life happening.

"I do not know exactly what I need. Can you help?"

Absolutely. This is one of the most common situations for first-time renters, and it is exactly why we are here. Call us at (858) 255-1130 and tell us about your event: how many guests, the type of event, your venue, and your budget. We will recommend exactly what you need and give you a complete quote.
